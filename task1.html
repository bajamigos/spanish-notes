SIELE S4 Task 1: Describing Visuals

Back to Resources

Task Description

For SIELE S4 Task 1, the student describes a complex image in Spanish to refine advanced vocabulary and descriptive fluency.

Exercise Outline

  1. Select a detailed image (e.g., a vibrant festival, urban scene, or cultural event) from Pinterest.
  2. Share the image via video call screen share and ask: "Describe en español esta imagen con el mayor detalle posible, incluyendo el ambiente, emociones o contexto cultural que transmite." (Allow 1–2 minutes.)
  3. If the student needs prompting, ask open-ended questions: "¿Qué historia podría contar esta imagen? ¿Qué elementos sugieren un contexto social o histórico específico?"
  4. In the Google Form (on the main page), log: (a) one pronunciation or grammar correction, (b) 2–3 advanced vocabulary words to elevate fluency (e.g., "vibrante - vibrant", "evocador - evocative").

Resources

Use Pinterest (search terms like “Spanish culture”, “Latin American festivals”) for rich, evocative images.
