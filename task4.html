SIELE S4 Task 4: Short Monologue

Back to Resources

Task Description

For SIELE S4 Task 4, the student delivers a 2-minute monologue in Spanish on a broad topic, practicing organized speech.

Exercise Outline

  1. Provide a topic: "Habla en español sobre un concepto, evento o idea que te interese."
  2. Allow 1–2 minutes for the student to speak.
  3. If they pause, ask: "¿Qué aspectos adicionales podrías explorar?" or "¿Cómo se conecta esto con otros temas?"
  4. In the Google Form, log: (a) one grammar or coherence correction, (b) 2–3 advanced connectors (e.g., "en primer lugar - firstly").

Resources

Select topics from diverse areas (e.g., current events, abstract ideas, or hypothetical scenarios).
