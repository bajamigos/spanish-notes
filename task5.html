SIELE S4 Task 5: Opinion Debate

Back to Resources

Task Description

For SIELE S4 Task 5, the student debates a thought-provoking topic in Spanish, practicing argumentation and justification.

Exercise Outline

  1. Select a recent news topic or engaging issue (e.g., "Should urban car use be restricted?", "Is AI overhyped for education?", or "Should social media platforms regulate misinformation?").
  2. Ask the student to open with their position: "En español, presenta tu argumento inicial sobre este tema, con justificaciones." (Allow 1 minute.)
  3. Argue the counter position in a couple of back-and-forth exchanges to challenge their views.
  4. Close by prompting the student for a summary: "En tu argumento final, organiza tus ideas usando expresiones sofisticadas y conectores."
  5. In the Google Form, log: (a) one advanced grammar correction (e.g., subjunctive), (b) 2–3 sophisticated expressions (e.g., "cabe destacar que - it’s worth noting that").
