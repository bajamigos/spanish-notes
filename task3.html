SIELE S4 Task 3: Role-Play Dialogue

Back to Resources

Task Description

For SIELE S4 Task 3, the student practices a simulated conversation in Spanish, focusing on interactive dialogue skills.

Exercise Outline

  1. Select a novel scenario (e.g., negotiating a business deal, resolving a customer complaint, or planning an event), and assign a specific role to the student (e.g., "Eres el gerente de una tienda" or "Eres un turista perdido").
  2. Assign a tone to the interaction (e.g., formal, hurried, friendly, annoyed, argumentative, sarcastic, empathetic, or authoritative) to add depth.
  3. Start the role-play with a Spanish prompt aligned to the tone: "¡Hola! Bienvenido al hotel. ¿En qué te ayudo?" (Continue for 2–3 minutes, responding to the student’s answers.)
  4. In the Google Form, log: (a) one pronunciation or grammar correction, (b) one specific error in their speech, (c) 1–2 advanced polite expressions (e.g., "me podría indicar - could you tell me").

Resources

Draw from everyday or professional contexts; encourage creativity in scenarios (e.g., inspired by real-world news or fictional twists).
