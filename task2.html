SIELE S4 Task 2: Responding to Media

Back to Resources

Task Description

For SIELE S4 Task 2, the student listens to a short audio/video clip and responds in Spanish, practicing quick comprehension and expression.

Exercise Outline

  1. Choose a 20–30-second Spanish audio/video clip (e.g., YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, news snippet, or podcast segment).
  2. Play the clip via screen share and ask: "En español, resume lo que escuchaste o da tu opinión." (Allow 1 minute.)
  3. Follow up with targeted questions to draw out specialized vocabulary: "¿Cómo se relaciona esto con temas actuales en tecnología/salud/cultura?" or "¿Qué implicaciones tiene para la sociedad?"
  4. In the Google Form, log: (a) one grammar or fluency correction, (b) a conjugation error, (c) 1–2 advanced connectors (e.g., "en consecuencia - as a result").
